Introduction

Inkfish Cosmetics

Inkfish cosmetics is an ethically sourced makeup brand that aims to bring high quality, all-day wear makeup to those who want to change the world. Dare to be bold and bright with everything you do, including your cosmetics. Customize your style by using our “Build Your Own Palette” feature to create your personalized color palette with help from our professional team and Complementary Color Quiz.

Target audience

The target audience is young adult females and males, aged 15-35 already invested in the cosmetic industry that want a makeup brand that is both unique and ethically sourced. The brand will equally target both males and females, supporting ending gender stereotypes within the cosmetic industry.

Goals for Inkfish Cosmetics include creating makeup lines with real, safe ingredients that customers can express themselves with and creating a range of products that appeal to to everyone. Inkfish Cosmetics wants to have the customer be able to build their own unique style by using our ‘Build Your Own Palette” feature. Inkfish wants every customer to feel special and celebrated.
